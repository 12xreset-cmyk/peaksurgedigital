Transparency

Why is there a one-time setup fee?

Before a single post ever goes live, there is a significant amount of real, skilled work that happens behind the scenes. The setup fee covers every hour of that foundational work — and it is what separates a professional agency engagement from someone who just starts posting randomly with no strategy. Here is exactly what your setup fee covers:

Full Platform Audit

We review every existing social media profile, Google Business listing, and online presence before touching anything. We identify exactly what is working, what is broken, and what is missing entirely.

Brand Kit Creation

We build a complete visual identity system — your exact color palette, typography, logo usage rules, and graphic templates. Every post we create uses this system so your brand looks consistent and professional across every platform, every time.

Profile Optimization

Every profile gets rewritten from scratch — bio, category, contact info, links, highlights, and cover images — all optimized for local search and first impressions. This alone is worth the setup fee.

Content Strategy Build

We develop your content pillars, posting schedule, tone of voice, and the specific angles that will resonate with your audience. This is the strategic foundation everything else is built on.

First Content Calendar

Your first full month of content is planned, designed, written, and ready for your approval before we ever charge you a monthly retainer. You see exactly what you are getting before the recurring billing begins.

Platform Setup & Linking

Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile are all connected, cross-linked, and configured correctly so content flows efficiently and nothing falls through the cracks.

The bottom line: The setup fee is not a barrier to entry. It is the investment that ensures everything we build for your business is done correctly from day one — so the monthly retainer actually delivers results instead of just filling a content calendar with random posts.
